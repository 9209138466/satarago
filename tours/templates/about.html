Who We Are

Making Satara Accessible to Everyone

We started with a simple observation: Tourists love Satara, but local transport is confusing and unreliable. SataraGo was born to fix that.

Our Startup Journey

1. The Idea 💡

Noticed tourists struggling to find reliable cabs for Kaas and Thoseghar.

2. Research & Plan 🔍

Spent months talking to local drivers, hotel owners, and tourists to understand the exact pain points.

3. Launch 🚀

Built the simplest version of SataraGo and launched with 5 trusted local drivers.

4. Onboarding Users 👥

Helped our first 100 tourists explore Satara without a single bargaining argument!

5. Improve 🛠️

Added live tracking, fixed pricing, and better vehicle options based on customer feedback.

6. Growth 📈

Expanding to cover every hidden gem in the Satara district, from forts to waterfalls.

7. The Future 🌟

Aiming to empower local drivers financially and put Satara on the map as a digital-first tourist hub.
